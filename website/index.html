Friday Oct. 21st: Poelking Lanes South

Start Time: 7:00 pm. Reminder there is a $15 fee at the door to cover lane rental and add to the prize fund. The league is open to everyone ages 18 and up. However, you must sign up first. This can be done by clicking Sign-Up in the navigation tab above. Bowlers are expected to have their own equipment and shoes. The format is three individual games played at the start to determine placing in head to head games afterward, which are best of 3. There will be a winner every week who will recieve $50, but this doesn't determine your placing overall.
